ABOUT ME


When i was young

In high school, i was the kid that seemed to never pay attention. Seemingly in my own world of designs. i've had a pencil in my hand since i was in diapers. Things never change... I'm no longer in diapers though.

Opening a door to the future

When i started becode i originally had the idea of going into full stack development. After building my first website, i was hooked. But i knew it was not 100 procent what i wanted. So i founded out about ux/ui. i ended my class and started with ux design. I am so happy that i made that decision.

The love

I do the work i do because i love it. I like the industry. I like the people i meet. I embrace the nerd. I like to make the web a better place. Hopefully we cross paths in the real world someday and we work together so we can leave the world better than we found it.

The struggles

That doesn't mean i don't have struggles. I stutter with my words, I have gill de la tourette, many people don't believe in me and think i should just quit. but the only thing that matters is that you keep believing that one day your dream will come true. it will!
